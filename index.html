FlowPolicy: Enabling Fast and Robust 3D Flow-based Policy via Consistency Flow Matching for Robot Manipulation

Results on Adroit

We present the results of the proposed FlowPolicy on Adroit. The Adroit environment contains three dexterity tasks which are Hammer, Door and Pen. In Adroit environment, the tasks focus on dexterous manipulation using a robotic hand. The challenges involve precise control and coordination of multiple fingers to achieve specific goals.
